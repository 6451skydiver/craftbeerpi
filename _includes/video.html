Hardware

The CraftBeerPI has very small hardware requirements

Raspberry PI
 

The software is running on every Raspberry PI Model.

1-Wire Digital Temperatursensor

1-Wire Temperatursensor (DS1820) can be connected to observe the Kettle temperature

Relays
 

Relay boards or solid state relais (SSR) can be connected to control the heater or agitator
